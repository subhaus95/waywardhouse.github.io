## Why this matters

Regression allows us to estimate mathematical relationships from data and use those relationships for prediction.

## Linear regression

$$
y = ax + b
$$

Regression determines parameters a and b that best fit observed data.

## Least squares idea

$$
\sum (y_i - \hat{y}_i)^2
$$

Minimizing this quantity produces the best‑fitting line.

## Geography example

Hydrologists often use regression models to estimate river discharge from rainfall measurements.
