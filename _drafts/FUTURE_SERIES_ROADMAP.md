## Series 6: Atmospheric Hazards & Dynamics (PLANNED)
**Total planned:** ~13 essays across 4 clusters

### Cluster Q: Fire Science
- Fire Weather Indices (FWI, drought codes, Canadian FWI system)
- Smoke Dispersion modelling (plume dynamics, atmospheric transport)
- Fire Spread Algorithms (Rothermel equation, FARSITE, ember transport)

### Cluster R: Severe Weather
- Tornado Path Prediction (vorticity, mesocyclone detection, damage assessment)
- Hurricane Track Forecasting (ensemble models, storm surge, intensity prediction)
- Hail Formation (updraft strength, supercell dynamics, MESH algorithm)
- Atmospheric Instability Indices (CAPE, lifted index, severe weather parameters)

### Cluster S: Wind & Turbulence
- Wind Resource Mapping (power density, Weibull distribution, capacity factor)
- Turbulence Prediction (Richardson number, wind shear, aviation hazards)
- Downburst/Microburst Detection (outflow boundaries, divergence)

### Cluster T: Flood Hydrology
- Unit Hydrograph Theory (rainfall-runoff transformation, time of concentration)
- Flood Routing (Muskingum method, kinematic wave, hydraulic routing)
- Urban Drainage (rational method, curve numbers, peak discharge)
