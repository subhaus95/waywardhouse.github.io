## Why this matters

Two datasets can have the same average but behave very differently. Variance measures how spread out observations are around the mean.

## Variance

$$
Var(X) = E[(X-\mu)^2]
$$

## Standard deviation

$$
\sigma = \sqrt{Var(X)}
$$

Standard deviation expresses variability using the same units as the data.

## Geography example

Climate scientists track the variance of temperature anomalies to detect changes in climate variability.
